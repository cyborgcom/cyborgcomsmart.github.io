Privacy Policy for Contact0

Last updated: 2025-11-17

Contact0 ("the app") is developed by Cyborgcom. This policy explains how the app handles your information. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. The app has no backend servers operated by us.

Data we collect

None. Cyborgcom does not collect, receive, or store any personal data from users of Contact0. The app does not use third‑party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Backup to your personal cloud

Contact0 backs up your message templates and message history to your own cloud platform service associated with your device (for example, iCloud on iOS or Google Drive / Android Auto Backup on Android). This backup is performed by the operating system or your chosen cloud provider under your account. Cyborgcom does not host, access, or receive a copy of this data. The handling of that backup is governed by the privacy policy of your cloud provider.

You can disable or delete these backups at any time through your device or cloud account settings.

Notifications

The app may schedule local notifications on your device. No notification content is transmitted to external servers.

Permissions

  • Notifications – to deliver local reminders you schedule.

You can revoke this permission at any time in your device settings.

Children

Contact0 is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
